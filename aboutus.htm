About Us

Saudi Arab #1 Hypermarket Chain

Danube is a prominent supermarket and hypermarket chain in Saudi Arabia, known for its wide variety of products and premium offerings. It is part of BinDawood Holding and operates 55 locations across Saudi Arabia and Bahrain. Danube is recognized for its diverse product range, including fresh produce, bakery items, ready-to-eat options, and a wide selection of dairy products. It also features hand-picked imported goods, organic and natural products, and differentiated ranges.
