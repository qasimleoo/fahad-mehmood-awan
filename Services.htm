Our Services

Services We Offer

Danube Supermarket offers a variety of services, including online grocery shopping, home delivery, and in-store shopping. They also provide services like cash on delivery and have a strong focus on customer convenience through their online platform and app, Danube Online. Additionally, Danube Hypermarket is known for its wide selection of products, including fresh produce, household goods, and international items, all within a well-organized and spacious environment.

Online Grocery Shopping

Danube Online allows customers to order groceries and have them delivered to their doorstep.

Home Delivery

Danube provides both home delivery and store pickup options for customer convenience.

In-Store Shopping

Danube Supermarkets offer a wide range of products, including fresh produce, household goods, and international items, within a spacious and well-organized environment.

Our Facilities

Danube Supermarket facilities include a wide range of grocery and household items, including fresh produce, bakery items, and imported goods. They also offer services like online shopping with home delivery and have dedicated sections for specific needs like organic foods and electronics. Additionally, some locations provide amenities such as parking, prayer rooms, and even entertainment centers like the "Yabeela" at Al Liwan.

Grocery

Danube Supermarket offers a wide range of grocery items, including fresh produce, meat, seafood, bakery items, and imported goods. The supermarket features a bakery with fresh bread and sweets, as well as sections for electronics and other household items. Customers appreciate the store's cleanliness, organization, and the availability of both local and imported products at competitive prices. Additionally, Danube provides parking facilities, and some locations may have prayer rooms for customer convenience.

Bakery

Danube Bakery provides a variety of freshly baked goods, including cakes, pastries, and bread, which are prepared with high-quality ingredients.

HouseHold Items

Danube Supermarket offers a wide array of household items, including kitchenware, home decor, and cleaning supplies, in addition to its food and grocery offerings. They are known for their premium quality products and hand-picked, exclusive imported goods.

About Us

Danube Company Limited is a supermarket and hypermarket chain in Saudi Arabia. It is owned by BinDawood Holding, the grocery retail operator of hypermarkets and
supermarkets in Saudi Arabia.Since becoming part of BinDawood Holding in 2001, Danube has grown to 47 current locations.
